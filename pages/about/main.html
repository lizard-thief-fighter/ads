FAQ



What is Arte della Scherma?

We reconstruct martial arts from Renaissance fencing treatises.
Essentially, we fight each other with swords - but artfully, and safely.
We are woman, minority, and queer friendly, and do not tolerate bigotry.



Is it safe?

We use blunt trainers to simulate weapons and wear protective equipment.
This *is* a contact sport, but our first concern is with practicing safely.
We require signage of a safety waiver as an acknowledgement of risk.
